Attire

The climate in Lexington during August can average in the high eighties during the day and low sixties in the evening. The ceremony will be held outside and the reception will be in an temperature controlled space. The wedding attire is dressy casual; we recommend something that will be comfortable in the heat and an extra layer for when it gets cooler in the evening.

Transportation

We will provide a shuttle service to and from the venue at the two hotels we have arranged blocks through. Sign-up sheets will be available at the front desk for both guests staying at the hotel along with those staying elsewhere but wishing to utilize the service. As the evening winds down shuttles will run on 30 minute intervals dropping off at each of the two hotels. Guests are also welcome to drive to the venue where valet parking will be provided.

Travel

For guests traveling from out of state there are two nearby airports, the Charlottesville-Albemarle (CHO) and Roanoke Regional (ROA), each located about an hour from Lexington. Links to directions for both hotels from each airport are located below:

  • Charlottesville -> Wyngate
  • Charlottesville -> Holiday Inn Express
  • Roanoke -> Wyngate
  • Roanoke -> Holiday Inn Express

Accommodations

We have reserved room blocks at two hotels located nearest to the venue, The Wyngate and The Holiday Inn Express. Please make your reservation over the phone and mention The Bisset/Presnell Wedding to take advantage of the discounted rate.

There are also a great selection of local hotels, bed & breakfasts, and Airbnb listings available should you prefer to stay elsewhere.

Registry

We are so excited to share our wedding day with our friends and family. Because we already share a home together our material needs are fewer. If you're inclined to get us a gift to help celebrate, in addition to a normal registry we have also included registry funds for experiences we can share as a married couple. You can find our registries at Newlywish and Amazon.
